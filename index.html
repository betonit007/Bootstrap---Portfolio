About Me

Thanks for stopping by and looking at my Portfolio. My full name is Timothy Michael James Nagorski, but you can just call me Tim...or as my friends sometimes call me: "Timmyah!" I currently live in Morrisville, NC with my beautiful wife, Susan, and our two boys, Steven and Luke. I lived in the Raleigh area for just over 20 years, but am originally from Kirkwood, NY, a small town located in upstate New York about an hour east of Syracuse.

I currently own and operate a small drone business named TSL Aerial. We specialize in aerial photography, 3d mapping, and insurance claim inspections. I operate out of the RDU area and service most of North Carolina. I have over 20 years of experience with UAS (Unmanned Aerial System) vehicles and drones. I also design, build and repair many types of aerial platforms.
